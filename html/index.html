How do I change my account email?

You can log in to your account and change it from your Profile > Edit Profile. Then go to the general tab to change your email.

What should I do if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, you can use the (COD) payment option, if available on that order. If your payment is debited, it will be credited back within 7-10 days.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can now cancel an order when it is packed/shipped. Any amount paid will be credited into the same payment mode within 7-10 days.

How do I check order delivery status?

Please tap on "My Orders" section under the main menu to check your order status.

What is Instant Refunds?

Upon successful return, Mytika will refund to your source account or chosen method instantly. It is not available in a few select pin codes.

How do I apply a coupon on my order?

You can apply a coupon before placing your order in the cart. Valid coupons are available under "My Coupons" section.
